# Regular Expressions:


### email:

 /^([a-z0-9_\.\+-]+)@([\da-z\.-]+)\.([a-z\.]{2,6})$/

### HTML tag:

/^<([a-z]+)([^<]+)*(?:>(.*)<\/\1>|\s+\/>)$/

### IP adress:

/^(?:(?:25[0-5]|2[0-4][0-9]|[01]?[0-9][0-9]?)\.){3}(?:25[0-5]|2[0-4][0-9]|[01]?[0-9][0-9]?)$/

### URL:

/^(https?:\/\/)?([\da-z\.-]+)\.([a-z\.]{2,6})([\/\w \.-]*)*\/?$/

### Slug:

/^[a-z0-9-]+$/
